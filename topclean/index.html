TopClean+

A cash flow and operations app for service businesses. Built for a cleaning business in Benin that was bleeding cash, now a template for any small business whose books don't match the bank.

The problem

Two years of quiet cash leakage

A cleaning and laundry business in Benin, 21 employees, around 10 million FCFA a month in revenue on a good month. One receptionist handled the money AND recorded the transactions. No independent verification. No photo trail. No end-of-day reconciliation.

For two years, cash walked out the door. The owner knew it was happening. He couldn't prove it. He couldn't stop it without firing people he wasn't sure were guilty.

What it does

Separates who touches cash from who records it, by design

Transaction entry

Every ticket has a client, service, amount, advance payment, payment mode, and an optional photo. The receptionist can't silently skim because every transaction has a paper trail.

Expense tracking

Every expense requires a mandatory photo of the receipt. No more phantom expenses.

Daily closing

End-of-day cash count plus mobile money reconciliation. The app auto-flags discrepancies between reported and counted amounts.

Owner dashboard

Live stats, payment method splits, pending approvals, service-by-service progress bars. The owner sees the whole business on one screen.

French chat assistant

Natural language in French. Staff can create tickets or expenses just by typing. No training required.

Weekly Excel export

Four sheets: Tickets, Depenses, Bilan Hebdo, Clotures. For the accountant, the bank, or the tax filing.

Why it works

A design decision, not just a feature list

TopClean+ doesn't catch thieves. It removes the opportunity to steal without leaving a trace. Every cash-touching action requires a photo, a timestamp, or a reconciliation step. The receptionist still handles the money, but the accountability trail lives outside of their hands.

Built intentionally lightweight. localStorage, PWA, no backend, no login drama. Two roles: Patron (owner) and Personnel (staff). Four-digit PIN auth. Works offline. Installs on any phone.
